Heinrich schutz (1585-1672)

germany
Studied music with his innkeeper father
Landgrave moritz von hessenkassel heard 13 year old heinrich singing and asked his father to be in charge of his music career
Heinrich graduated with honors in 1608
Langrave gave heinrich a grant to travel to venice where he studied composition until 1913
Heinrich became court composer for the elector of saxony in dresden in 1615.
Heinrich was in this position for the rest of his career
During the 30 years war he studied with claudio monteverdi in venice and served as kapellmeisterto king christian IV of denmark for several years
Heinrich is credited with bring the italianate style to germany due to his studies in italy
